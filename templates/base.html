Welcome to SwishApp!

SwishApp is your go-to platform for organizing and participating in sports matches in your local community. Whether you're into basketball, volleyball, football, handball, tennis, or ping-pong, SwishApp makes it easy to find, join, and manage matches.

Features:
-Add Matches: Easily create matches for your favorite sports, specify the day, time, and location, and invite others to join.
-Match Calendar: Keep track of your planned matches with our intuitive calendar view. Never miss a game again!
-User Profiles: Manage your sports activities and preferences in one place. See the matches you've added and the ones you've planned to join.
-Find Courts: Discover courts near you with detailed information on facilities and availability.
-Community Engagement: Connect with other sports enthusiasts in your area. Share your passion and improve your skills through regular games.

SwishApp is designed to foster a vibrant sports community, making it easier than ever to stay active and engaged. Join us and start playing today!
